About the Avengers

The Avengers are a team of superheroes, appearing in comic books published by Marvel Comics. The team first appeared in "The Avengers" #1 in 1963, created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby. The Avengers consist of some of the most iconic and powerful heroes in the Marvel Universe, joining forces to combat threats that no single hero can defeat.

Members

The core members of the Avengers include:

  • Iron Man (Tony Stark)
  • Captain America (Steve Rogers)
  • Thor (Thor Odinson)
  • Hulk (Bruce Banner)
  • Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff)
  • Hawkeye (Clint Barton)

Mission

The Avengers are dedicated to protecting the world from any threat, whether it be terrestrial or cosmic. They strive to uphold justice and safeguard humanity against any peril that might arise.
